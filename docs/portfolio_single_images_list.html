Description

Stashd simplifies online shopping and has introduced the process of 'browsing' to the digital space, resulting in high levels of consumer engagement on a global scale.

From a technical perspective, Stashd presents the excitement of building world class retail intelligence, with the challenge of doing so at scale- developing- managing and maintaining a database with over 700,000 individual items.

Stashd has users in 140 countries, has been featured within Apples Best New Apps in 11 countries and within the top 10 Lifestyle Apps in 5 countries.

Stashd has been seen in publications such as Forbes, The Australian, Channel 7 News, GQ, and Cleo just to name a few.
