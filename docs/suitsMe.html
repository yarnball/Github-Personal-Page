Description

SuitsMe was developed in 2013. It is a brand and website which sells tailor made suits.

The custom built website allows the user to be as free as they want in designing their suit. The "SuitBuilder" caters to users depending on what level of customisation they want to go into. Users can simply order a ready-designed suit, choose the middle option which allows some customisation (such as colour) or choose the fully-bespoke option- which allows the customer to customise every last detail of their suit.

The platform provided a unique and streamlined experience in browsing, ordering and customising award winning tailor made suits.

The site contained 3D interactive models to aid customers in visualising the purchase, and an intuitive measuring process.

"Made by you, for you".
